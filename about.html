About Me

Education

I am a graduate of Advanced Diploma in Digital Design at The Malta College of Arts, Science & Technology. The program has provided me with a strong foundation in interactive media, graphic design principles, and game art. Some of the notable units I have completed include:

  • Introduction to Interactive Media
  • Graphic Design Fundamentals
  • User Interface Design


Achievements

  • Advanced Diploma in Digital Design (2023)
  • Diploma in Media (2021)
  • Advanced Levels in English and Maltese (2019)

Design Philosophy

I believe that good design should not only be visually appealing but also functional and user-centred. I strive to create designs that look great and provide a seamless and intuitive user experience. I approach each project with a combination of creativity, research, and attention to detail, ensuring that the final product meets the client's goals and exceeds user expectations.



Personal Interests

Outside of my studies and work, I enjoy exploring various hobbies and interests. Some of my personal interests include exercise, crafts, true crime documentaries, and learning about European history. I believe that diverse experiences and knowledge contribute to my creative process and help me bring unique perspectives to my design work.
