Web Design Assignment

I built this blog website as part of my Web Design unit. The brief required analysis of existing websites that are similar to my desired project, a survey to sum up the average user feedback, idea generation to come up with a style guide and a design plan, wireframes, and finally coding.

(https://theblissfulmind.com/)
(https://www.attiremedia.com/)
(https://myparisianlife.com/)

Through the surveys, it was concluded that the average customer is 22 years old and frequently uses social media such as Instagram, Tiktok, and Facebook. It was also revealed that the average user has browsed blog websites before and would expect contact information, a menu bar, high quality media, and easy navigation. They admired the simplicty and the use of thumbnails in the websites that served as inspiration for me. However, they found some of them to be disorganised and one of them to have an unlegible font.

Moving on to the site map, I jotted down all of the sections and tabs that each of the three websites above contained on a notebook. I then eliminated those which were deemed repetitive or unnecessary for my own website. My aim was to maintain the simplicity aspect as mindfulness is all about calm and declutter.

This led me to develop my style guide, which was inspired by examples found online. This included the logo design, the colour palette that I might use, and the typefaces and fonts for each text.

Also included in this style guide are samples of buttons that I could use, and the social media icons. All of this was done using Adobe Illustrator before a mock-up on Figma was designed.

The wireframing part was the most challenging as the components had to be extremely simplistic and there were many options on how to approach this blog website. In fact, as I was coding, I adjusted some of the layout of these wireframes to something more functional and easy to navigate. The above are some examples of the wireframes that I submitted.
